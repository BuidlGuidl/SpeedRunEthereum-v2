# Image-generation prompts — V4 intro illustrations

Optional pixel-art stills to upgrade the V4 intro. Drop finished images into
`packages/remotion/public/` and tell Claude the filenames — each one is a
one-line `staticFile()` swap in `src/WebAIAssistantLaunch.tsx`.

Palette to keep consistent across all three (matches `src/theme.ts` and the
existing sprites): teal `#088484`, light teal `#67DDDE`, pale cyan `#C8F5FF`,
cream `#f7feff`, dark teal text `#026262`.

---

## Asset 1 — "The Wall" (biggest impact — Beat A/B, replaces the coded wall + builder-fronzen.png)

Wide 16:9, ideally 1920×1080.

> Pixel art, 16:9 widescreen. A tiny builder character with a teal Ethereum
> hard hat stands at the bottom center, seen from behind, dwarfed by an
> enormous towering wall made of stacked glowing documents and dense text
> blocks that leans over him. Teal and cream color palette (#088484, #67DDDE,
> #C8F5FF, #f7feff), soft gradient sky, clean retro game aesthetic, dramatic
> scale contrast, moody but not dark, no readable words or letters anywhere.

## Asset 2 — "Unblocked" (Beat C / outro)

Wide or square, transparent or cream background.

> Pixel art. The same builder character with a teal Ethereum hard hat, now
> relaxed and smiling, chatting with a small friendly floating robot assistant
> made of teal sparkles. Two or three empty speech bubbles float between them.
> Bright, optimistic mood, teal and cream palette (#088484, #67DDDE, #C8F5FF,
> #f7feff), clean retro game aesthetic, simple background, no readable text.

## Asset 3 — Engraved teal clouds (optional — replaces the square particles behind intro/outro titles)

PNG with transparent background, 2–3 separate cloud shapes.

> Vintage engraving-style illustration of fluffy cumulus clouds, fine
